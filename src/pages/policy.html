Trix Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how Trix collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information when you use our platform. By accessing or using Trix, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

  • a. Personal Information: When you register for a Trix account or use our services, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, billing information, and other details necessary for account creation and transactions.
  • b. Usage Data: We may also collect usage data such as your IP address, device information, browser type, and interactions with our platform to improve user experience and analyze trends.

How We Use Your Information

  1. a. Providing Services: We use your information to provide and personalize our services, process transactions, and communicate with you regarding your account and transactions.
  2. b. Improving User Experience: We analyze usage data to improve our platform's functionality, content, and user interface, as well as to develop new features and services.
  3. c. Marketing and Promotions: With your consent, we may send promotional offers, newsletters, and marketing communications about Trix products and services or those of our partners.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

  1. a. Third-Party Services: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform, processing payments, conducting analytics, and delivering marketing communications.
  2. b. Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information when required by law, legal proceedings, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or those of our users or others.

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices and Rights

  1. a. Account Information: You can review and update your account information at any time by logging into your Trix account.
  2. b. Marketing Preferences: You can choose to opt out of receiving marketing communications from Trix by adjusting your notification settings or contacting us directly.
  3. c. Data Access and Deletion: You have the right to request access to your personal information held by Trix and to request deletion of your information, subject to legal and contractual obligations.

Children's Privacy

Trix does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by email or through our platform. Continued use of Trix after such changes constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
